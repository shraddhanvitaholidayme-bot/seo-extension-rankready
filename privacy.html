Privacy Policy for RankReady Chrome Extension

RankReady does not collect any personal or sensitive information from users. All analysis is performed locally in your browser. No content or browsing data is sent to any server.

Permissions Used

  • activeTab: Required to read content of the currently active web page for analysis.
  • scripting: Required to inject the content script into the active tab for real-time analysis.

Data Collection

RankReady does not collect, store, or transmit any user data. Your browsing history and page content remain private and are processed only locally.
